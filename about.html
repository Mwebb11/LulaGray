Our Story

“we believe that your hair is not only an expression of yourself to the world but also an art form. The truest interpretation has the ability to provide confidence and empowerment”

At Lula Gray Salon,We strive to provide you with not only the highest quality of service but a look that truly captures who you are. Our stylists are committed to continuing education to stay up to date on the newest trends and technologies while maintaining a strong foundation in classic style. This is our goal and the reason we love coming to work every day! We know how daunting it can be walking into a new salon for the first time, and how little time we all get to spend on ourselves. So we wanted to create an environment where you feel comfortable and get the most out of your bit of ‘me time’, helping you to feel positive, confident and ready to take on the day!

Why the name Lula Gray?

When talking with my grandmother, Lula Jane, one day she told me the story of how she had originally named my mother, Lu Gray but my grandfather decided to change her birth certificate to Sue Carol instead! I have loved the name Lula Gray ever since and didn’t have a daughter to carry on the legacy, so I naturally had to name my other “baby”, the salon, Lula Gray!

Product Lines

The products we carry at Lula Gray Salon have been carefully selected to provide our guests with the ability to create their unique and personalized looks at home. We strive to provide balance between form and function so that you will look just as great on the day you return to get refreshed as you did the day you walked out of our doors. We look forward to seeing you soon!
